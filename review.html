Review: Kampung Bawang Assan, Sibu

Kampung Bawang Assan is an Iban village located in Sibu, Sarawak, Malaysia. Known for its traditional longhouses and rich cultural heritage, the village offers a unique glimpse into the Iban way of life. The village showcases the Iban culture through its longhouses, communal living spaces that reflect the community's traditions and architectural skills. Visitors are often greeted with traditional music, dance, and ceremonies.

The Iban people are known for their warm hospitality, making visitors feel welcome and involved in their daily activities and traditions.

Kampung Bawang Assan is a must-visit for those interested in Iban culture. Its longhouses, cultural performances, and natural beauty provide a memorable and authentic experience.
